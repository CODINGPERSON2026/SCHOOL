School Rules & Regulations 2026–27

Guidelines & Discipline Policy

  • Students must attend school regularly and be punctual.
  • Proper school uniform is compulsory on all working days.
  • Students must carry their ID cards daily.
  • Respect towards teachers, staff, and fellow students is mandatory.
  • Mobile phones and electronic gadgets are strictly prohibited.
  • Homework and assignments must be completed on time.
  • Damage to school property will result in disciplinary action.
  • Participation in school activities and events is encouraged.
  • Leave applications must be submitted in advance.
  • Any misconduct may lead to suspension or expulsion.
