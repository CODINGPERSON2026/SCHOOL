About S.R.V Public School

Inspiring Excellence • Building Character • Creating Future Leaders

Discover S.R.V Public School

Welcome to S.R.V Public School — a nurturing space where young minds are ignited with curiosity, guided by values, and empowered to shape a brighter tomorrow.

Established with a deep commitment to holistic education, S.R.V Public School (located in Rathaura, Mohammadabad, Farrukhabad, Uttar Pradesh) serves students from Nursery through Class 9 under a progressive CBSE-aligned curriculum. We blend strong academic foundations with character development, discipline, creativity, and life skills — preparing children not just for exams, but for life.

At the heart of our philosophy lies the belief that every child is unique. Our dedicated educators create personalized learning experiences in a safe, supportive, and joyful environment. Beyond textbooks, we emphasize co-curricular activities, sports, arts, moral education, and community values — helping students grow into confident, responsible, and compassionate individuals.

Parents choose S.R.V because we partner with families to unlock each child's full potential. Together, we build not only bright futures but also strong roots grounded in respect, integrity, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional, value-based education that equips every student with knowledge, critical thinking, emotional strength, and ethical grounding — empowering them to thrive in an ever-changing world.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier institution of learning that inspires innovation, fosters leadership, and nurtures principled global citizens who contribute meaningfully to society.
